Nova OS/SP ​

Quiver ​

⭐ Quiver SUBSUME Cloak Arrow: The Warframe that receives this ability can shoot an arrow that creates a zone of invisibility for itself and its allies. This is particularly useful for making mobs aggro on you and not shoot from a distance, causing them to stay in a fixed spot and not move.

  • Narrow Minded,Primed Continuity,Augur Message HIGH DURATION
  • Corrosive Projection to reduce the armor
  • We can replace per : Steel Charge / Melee Guidance / Growing Power/Dreamer’s Bond
  • Equilibrium / Primed Flow for energies
  • Precision Intensify ➛ 75% Speed Enemies
  • Vazarin - Zenurik Focus : Vazarin ➛ Protective Sling for immunity from damage Magus Cloud
  • Arcane : Molt Efficiency (more duration) Arcane Energize(Energy) Pulse is good for Nekros
  • Archon Shard : 5x Red Ability Duration

Rage ​

⭐ Rage SUBSUME A target affected by Rage has its speed increased and becomes more vulnerable to damage Use a light color to apply Rage; otherwise, you will put enemies to sleep instead of speeding them up

  • Narrow Minded,Primed Continuity,Augur Message HIGH DURATION
  • Corrosive Projection to reduce the armor
  • Can be replaced by : Steel Charge / Melee Guidance /Growing Power
  • Equilibrium / Primed Flow for energies
  • Umbral Intensify / Blind Rage ➛ 75% Speed Enemies and boost “Rage”

  • Vazarin/Zenurik Focus : Vazarin ➛ Protective Sling for immunity from damage Magus Cloud Zenurik ➛ WellSpring for energy restore

  • Arcane : Molt Efficiency (more duration) Arcane Steadfast(Energy efficiency) Power Ramp is ok to get the 75%

  • Archon Shard : 5x Red Ability Duration
