Companion ​

The beast Companions can also increase your earnings with their mods, which are as follows ;

  • One mod that can double your resources
  • One whome double credits
  • One that can double both, but with a lower percentage. We can use these mods on all beasts companions, but we can only equip one mod at a time.

NOTE

  • The one that will interest us the most is [Resourceful Retriever], which gives an 18% chance to double pickups.
  • Since the nerf to Smeeta and its Charm mod, the companion that now makes the most sense to use is the Chesa Kubrow.

Chesa Kubrow ​

Chesa Build ​

  • “Retrieve” is a Kubrow exclusive mod that allows the Chesa Kubrow to retrieve additional items from enemy corpses or containers
  • Additional loot retrieval stacks with other loot abilities that loot on death (Khora Pilfering Strangledome), however, it does not stack with other loot corpse abilities Nekros "Desecrate"
  • “Calculated Redirection” to activate “Reinforced Bound”

A quick clarification regarding Nekros and the Chesa Kubrow :

INFO

It is true that Nekros Desecrate and the Chesa's Retrieve ability do not directly stack on the same corpse.

In other words, an enemy cannot be "doubly" looted by both abilities. However, these two abilities remain complementary :

Desecrate cannot trigger on every enemy (e.g., if the corpse disappears too quickly or has already been affected).

This is where the Chesa comes into play ; it can activate Retrieve on the corpses that Nekros might have missed.

Even with two Nekros in the squad, it’s impossible to hit 100% of the corpses in all situations.

Conclusion: Using a Chesa alongside a Nekros can improve overall loot coverage , without increasing the chances per corpse, but reducing potential losses .
