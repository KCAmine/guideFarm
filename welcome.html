Welcome to the Ultimate Nano Poly Ferrite Farming Guide ​

What to Expect ​

In this comprehensive guide, you will find everything you need to efficiently farm Nano Spores, Polymers, and Ferrite in Warframe. From optimal mission selection to squad composition and build recommendations, this guide provides clear and actionable strategies for each resource. It also includes detailed map analysis and tips to maximize farming efficiency.

Who This Guide Is For ​

This guide is intended for players of all experience levels. Beginners will benefit from structured strategies and explanations, while experienced players can use it as a reference for optimizing farming runs and experimenting with advanced techniques. Builds and strategies are presented as templates to adapt to your playstyle and squad setup.

How to Use This Guide ​

Use the menu on the left to navigate through the different sections, including resource-specific strategies, map layouts, recommended builds, and advanced tips. Each section is self-contained, allowing you to access the information you need quickly without unnecessary distractions. For best results, combine multiple sections to plan and execute highly efficient farming runs.
